Who we are

Steps of Joy Pediatric Physical Therapy is a dedicated pediatric practice focused on helping children reach their physical milestones in a supportive, family-centered environment.

We partner with parents and caregivers to understand your child’s unique strengths and challenges. Whether your little one needs help with early motor skills, recovery after an injury, or support for a developmental condition, we’re here with clear communication and compassionate guidance every step of the way.

Services

Care is individualized to your child’s age, goals, and everyday routines—at home, school, or in our clinic.

  • Gross motor & mobility

    Crawling, walking, running, jumping, and safe participation in play and sports—with attention to alignment and endurance.

  • Developmental support

    Guidance when milestones feel delayed or uneven, with activities you can practice between sessions.

  • Torticollis & plagiocephaly

    Gentle positioning, stretching, and parent education for infants with head or neck preferences.

  • Balance & coordination

    Exercises and games that build body awareness, stability, and confidence on the move.

  • Post-injury & post-surgical rehab

    Age-appropriate rehab plans to help kids return to what they love safely and gradually.

  • Equipment & home programs

    Recommendations for braces, orthotics, or adaptive tools when needed, plus a practical home exercise plan.

Our approach

Therapy should feel motivating—not intimidating. We use play, positive reinforcement, and goals that matter to your child and your family.

  • One-on-one sessions with clear, parent-friendly explanations
  • Evidence-based techniques adapted to your child’s personality
  • Collaboration with pediatricians and other providers when needed
  • Celebration of progress, no matter how small it may seem
